Help
Debit Note

A debit note increases the amount owed by the customer. Use this for additional charges or premium increases.

Credit Note

A credit note reduces the amount owed or creates a refund. Use this for premium refunds, cancellations, or discounts.

Common Reasons
  • Premium Adjustment: Change in premium due to endorsement
  • Endorsement: Policy change resulting in premium difference
  • Cancellation: Policy cancellation refund
  • Refund: General refund
  • Additional Coverage: Added coverage mid-term
  • Tax Adjustment: Tax recalculation
