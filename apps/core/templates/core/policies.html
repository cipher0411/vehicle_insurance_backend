How We Handle Your Policy

1. Instant Issuance

Your policy is issued immediately after payment. No waiting, no paperwork—just instant coverage with a digital certificate delivered to your dashboard and email.

2. Active Protection

From the moment your policy activates, you're fully protected. We monitor your coverage 24/7 and send reminders before renewal to ensure continuous protection.

3. Proactive Management

We keep you informed with timely notifications about your policy status, upcoming renewals, and exclusive offers tailored to your coverage needs.

4. Seamless Renewal

Renewing is effortless. We pre-fill your information, apply loyalty discounts automatically, and send your new certificate instantly upon payment.
