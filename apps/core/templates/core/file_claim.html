Our Simple 4-Step Claims Process

1. Report the Incident

Contact us immediately through our 24/7 hotline, online portal, or mobile app. We'll guide you on immediate next steps.

2. Submit Documentation

Upload photos, police report (if applicable), and any other relevant documents through our secure portal.

3. Claim Assessment

Our claims team reviews your submission and may arrange a vehicle inspection. We keep you updated throughout.

4. Settlement

Once approved, payment is processed quickly—typically within 48 hours of approval.

What to Do Immediately After an Incident

Ensure Safety First

Check for injuries and move to a safe location if possible. Call emergency services if needed.

Document Everything

Take photos of the scene, damage, and other vehicles involved. Note the time and location.

Exchange Information

Get contact and insurance details from other parties involved. Get witness contacts if available.

Contact Us Immediately

Call our 24/7 claims hotline or file online. We'll guide you through the next steps.
