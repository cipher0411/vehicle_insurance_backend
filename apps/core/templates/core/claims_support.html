Common Claims Questions

What should I do immediately after an accident?

1. Ensure everyone is safe and call emergency services if needed.
2. Take photos of the accident scene and vehicle damage.
3. Exchange information with other parties involved.
4. Call our 24/7 claims hotline immediately.
5. File a police report if required.

How long does the claims process take?

Most claims are settled within 48 hours of receiving complete documentation. Complex claims may take 5-7 business days. We keep you updated throughout the process.

What documents do I need to file a claim?

- Completed claim form
- Police report (for theft or major accidents)
- Photos of damage
- Driver's license
- Vehicle registration documents
- Repair estimates (2 quotes recommended)

Will filing a claim affect my premium?

Your No-Claims Bonus may be affected depending on the nature and frequency of claims. However, we offer claim protection add-ons that preserve your NCB even after a claim.
