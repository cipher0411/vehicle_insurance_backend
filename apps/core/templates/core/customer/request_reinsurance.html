What is Reinsurance?

Reinsurance provides additional protection by sharing the risk with a reinsurer. This ensures your claim will be paid even in cases of catastrophic events or large claims.

Benefits of Reinsurance:
  • Increased claim payment security
  • Protection against large catastrophic losses
  • Enhanced policy stability
  • Access to global reinsurance capacity

Frequently Asked Questions

Reinsurance adds a small additional premium based on the ceded amount. This premium is calculated as a percentage of the coverage amount ceded to the reinsurer. You'll earn commission on the reinsurance placement.

Reinsurance is typically bound for the policy period. If you need to make changes, please contact our support team to discuss your options.

Reinsurance ensures that funds are available for large claims, which can help expedite the claims process. Your claim handling remains with us, and we coordinate with the reinsurer behind the scenes.
Reinsurance Terms
Reinsurance Agreement

By requesting reinsurance, you acknowledge and agree that:

  • The reinsurance premium will be added to your outstanding balance
  • Reinsurance coverage is subject to the terms of the reinsurance treaty
  • Claims will be handled by your primary insurer with reinsurer participation
  • Reinsurance may affect policy renewal terms
  • Commission earned will be credited to your account
