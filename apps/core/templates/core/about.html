Our Story

Founded in 2020, VehicleInsure was born from a simple observation: getting vehicle insurance in Nigeria was too complicated, slow, and expensive.

Our founders, experienced insurance professionals and tech entrepreneurs, saw an opportunity to transform the industry using technology. They envisioned a platform where Nigerians could get instant quotes, purchase policies online, and file claims seamlessly - all from their mobile phones.

Today, VehicleInsure serves over 30,000 customers across all 36 states of Nigeria, processing thousands of policies monthly and settling claims within 48 hours on average. We're proud to be Nigeria's fastest-growing digital-first insurance provider.
