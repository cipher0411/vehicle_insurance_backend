Frequently Asked Questions

Which plan is best for a new car?

For new cars, we strongly recommend Comprehensive Coverage. This plan protects your investment against accidents, theft, fire, flood, and other risks. With a new vehicle, the peace of mind that comes with full protection is invaluable.

What's the difference between Comprehensive and Standalone?

Comprehensive includes both own-damage cover AND third-party liability protection. Standalone covers only damage to your own vehicle—it does NOT include third-party liability. Standalone is ideal if you already have third-party coverage elsewhere or want to supplement an existing policy.

Can I upgrade from Third Party to Comprehensive?

Yes! You can upgrade your coverage at any time. You'll only pay the pro-rated difference in premium for the remaining period of your policy. Contact our support team or visit your dashboard to upgrade.

Are there discounts for safe drivers?

Yes! We offer a No-Claims Bonus (NCB) that increases your discount for each claim-free year, up to 50% off your premium. The longer you drive safely, the more you save.

What does Personal Accident cover include?

Personal Accident coverage provides financial protection for you and your passengers in case of accidental death, permanent disability, or medical expenses resulting from an accident. It covers you regardless of who is at fault and includes hospital cash benefits and ambulance service cover.
